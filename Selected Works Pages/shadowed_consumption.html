Shadowed Consumption: Coming Soon...

Will be presented at The University of Maryland’s Spring 2025 IMD showcase.

In collaboration with Fiza Mulla

Artist Statement:

Shadowed Consumption 2D is a platformer game and installation that my talented partner and I will present at the University of Maryland’s Spring 2025 IMD showcase as our capstone project.

Shadowed Consumption is a single player game where the topic is on consumerism/over-consumption. The game starts with a character who goes to various different shopping places (online shopping on their phone through social media platforms/online shops, the grocery store, the mall, restaurant, coffee shop, etc). The game follows a storyline where the character has friends and lives in this world and travels to all these different locations and ultimately tries to obtain these items. After the item they want is introduced, each shopping location transforms into a platform game where the player must jump through/move through platforms to get the token/item. In the game, there is also a happiness level bar that tracks their happiness level. The plotline of the game is that the player is constantly feeling like they have to get more and more things to fit in and ultimately increase their happiness. However, the world around them slowly becomes affected by their choices and they do not see the affect it has on their own mental health.

Tools:

• Projection mapping
• Scene switching: buttons and triggers
• Dialogue generation
• AI enemy generation
• Platformer design
